    CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASE PREDICTION FOR NHS SUFFOLK & ESSEX

    In this project I analysed the current population vulnerable to/with cardiovascular disease in NHS Mid and East Essex and I also identified risk factors related to a stroke and a heart attack that could be used as predictors sing LASO REGRESSOR and correlation heat map. I predicted the number of people in Mid and South Essex who are likely to have a heart attack and the number of people who are likely to have a stroke in the next five years, as well as illustrating the CCG they are coming from.
